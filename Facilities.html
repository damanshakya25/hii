Facilities in Our School


Lord Mother Senior Secondry Public School, Bakewar, Etawah is an educational institution spreading the light of education to the students of rural areas irrespective of their sex, caste, creed and religion so that they learn to respect all religions and cultures, in our country and strive to achieve unity in diversity, as part of God's plan for humanity. It is an English Medium co-educational institution affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) New Delhi and is being run by Lord Lok Kalyan Samiti, Azad Nagar, Bakewar, Etawah U.P. (Regd.)

TRANSPORTATION SERVICE


GPS Tracking System

To ensure the safety of school students each bus has got the GPS system installed to track the safety and location of children while in transit anytime, anywhere which helps to provide the real time location to school management and parent.


Safety from home to school and back

  • High tech busses with attendants and GPS (Global Positioning System)
  • Trained and experienced drivers and escorts.
  • First aid kit in each bus.
  • Firefighting measures incorporated.
  • Punctuality (+15minutes) in arrival and departure from pickup point.


SAFETY AND SECURITY

Our primary responsibility is to ensure a safe and secure environment for the students, staff and visitors. School staff takes care of each child until the child is handed over to Parent/Guardian after proper verification. School is equipped with all the required security measures for protection from any untoward incident.


CCTV Surveillance

Our school has been equipped with video surveillance, CCTV systems to ensure safety and security students preventing outsiders from entering the school premise. These CCTV cameras are used for monitoring all activities of the school.
