House System




The students of the school are divided equally into four houses, namely Shivaji, Tagore, Ashoka, Raman through an organized distribution formula to get potential groups, so that a friendly competitive spirit Privail among the students round the session. Each House is headed by a House Master with the support of House Teachers. A powerful student wing is selected as discipline prefects and potential student leader is chosen as House Captain, who is supported by the members of each House.


The House System is a service-cum-training organization. Its objectives are.


  • To promote the welfare of the students.
  • To promote better students-teacher relationships.
  • To furnish citizenship training.
  • To encourage self-discipline.
  • To arouse greater school spirit.
  • To impart training in the skill of management and leadership

Houses perform the following duties in rotation.


  • To manage the Morning Assembly.
  • To check the late comers .
  • To help maintain order during the recess period.
  • To look after the general cleanliness of the school.
  • To check the student uniforms.
  • Participate enthusiastically in all inter house contests .
  • To help maintain discipline on the occasion of the school functions.
  • To help the school authorities in organizing various school function, and making suitable arrangements for them.
