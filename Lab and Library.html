Our Class Rooms


Computer Lab



Equipped with ther latest Pentium systems, the computer lab is in tune with the times. Apart from effectively aiding in teaching the prescribed syllabus, it endeavours to expose the latest technologies and skills to the students.


Laboratories



The school has spacious and well ventilated General Science, Physics,Chemistry, Biology Laboratory, furnished with all the necessary instruments and accessories, the labs ensure the practical understanding of the subject is comprehensive and complete.


Music Room



The school provides ample opportunities for students with an aptitude for music. Highly talented tutors and availability of different musical instruments help the budding musicians to pursue their interests with ease.


School Library



The in - house library with over 4000, e-books and a photocopying facility is an effective tool in the education process of the student. The collection of books range from pedagogic to philosophy. The library also subscribes to selected weekly and monthly journals, magazines and newsletters, apart from daily newspapers.


Audio - Visual 3D Classroom :



The 3D Classroom is awell furnishedand audio - visual set - up, It serves as a valuable educational aid and multimedia facility acting as a catalyst in not only creating interest but also in increasing understanding of thestudents in the basic concepts of various disciplines.


Games and Sport Facility



The Games and sport Amenities of the school range with games like cricket volleyball, kho kho, swimming, horse riding, carrom, chess etc. All the students are divided into four houses and inter - house competitions are held in all the games both for senior and junior students.
