VALUABLE THOUGHTS BY THE PILLARS OF THE SCHOOL

Mr. Upendra Sengar

(Chief Proctor)


Physical Education helps in developing students’ competence and confidence. It helps them to take part in a wide range of physical activities that are crucial to their lives— both in and out of school. As a Physical Education Tearcher, I found that games support the development of wide range of skills essential to the autonomous learner


The first benefit of physical education is that it teaches ethical traits that students need to thrive in this competitive world. The main ethics that they learn from physical education are perseverance and work ethics. Whether in physical education or sports, disputes often arise and only ethics and perseverance from the players can reduce the disputes.


Physical fitness is the key ingredient for a healthy lifestyle. This is why physical education is an important element in most schools. When students have regular fitness activities as part of their daily regimen, they stay fit and healthy. Regular physical activities daily help in better absorption of nutrients in the body and also helps in improving cardiovascular health and developing muscular strength


One of the advantages of physical education is that it helps in improving the academic performance of the students. Most physical activities that students do in schools are related to higher levels of concentration which in turn help with better behaviors. Physical activities and sports activities help in boosting the knowledge learned in the classroom.


Keeping all these things in view, we try to encourage every student in our school to take part in physical activities.



Mr. Gyanendra Singh Chauhan

(Exam Controller)


An examination system evaluates factual understanding, conceptual understanding and global understanding of prescribed syllabus and designed curriculum. Examination system assesses individual's capability to interpret learning of his own language. It is not merely test of knowledge. Examination system evaluates presistant and consistent quality of an individual. It tests the attitude of an individual i.e. how does an individual perform better in extreme pressure


Examination examines individual's overall personality. Examination systems examine individual's desire to learn, desire to explore desire to investigate and desire to apply learning in real life situation. Assessment ensure quality teaching learning process.


Exams are very important for children. Not just because they pass it and they will be promoted to the next class, but because in school they learn that something like an exam is like that and how is it handled. Instead of cheating in the exam without taking any pressure as a burden, If we can provide a good environment by motivating them, then only they will be able to face every difficulty with courage in their coming tomorrow.


I being the exam in - charge, always take care that every child of Lord Mother Public School should appear in the examination with true devotion and his moral should not broken by any such untoward incident. That is why I keep making constant effort that all types of examinations are conducted smoothly in the school.
