About Us

Lord Mother Senior Secondry Public School, Bakewar, Etawah is an educational institution spreading the light of education to the students of rural areas irrespective of their sex, caste, creed and religion so that they learn to respect all religions and cultures, in our country and strive to achieve unity in diversity, as part of God's plan for humanity. It is an English Medium co-educational institution affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) New Delhi and is being run by Lord Lok Kalyan Samiti, Azad Nagar, Bakewar, Etawah U.P. Education is the, foundation of all developments. It is for this very reason that we embraced upon the journey into education. Mission

"To develop in the students of Lord Mother Senior Secondary Public School the zeal to utilize their potential to the maximum as well as a mindset for excellence and nurture them to be a responsible and caring citizen. Vision

With a vision to 'Cultivate one Million children in india as Neoteric Innovators'. Atal Innovation Mission is establishing Atal Tinkering Laboratories (ATLs) sponsered by Niti Ayog (Govt. of India)in schools across India. The objective of this scheme is to foster curiosity, creativity, and imagination in young minds, and inculcate skills such as design mindset, computational thinking, adaptive learning etc.

Tour & Excursion

An Educational Tour or A Field Trip is a visit to a place away from their normalplace of study. The purpose of this trip is to provide students an experienceoutside the class rooms or labs... While on an educational tour/field tripstudents have the opportunity to have lively discussion in an informal set up. Keeping this view our school has organized a educational tour or excursionsevery year.
