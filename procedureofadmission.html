Admission Procedure







Fill up the registration form submit in the office and take the date of interaction / Entrance test. Bring the child as per instructions on specific date. See the selection list on a date notified by the office. Fill up the admission form and deposit in the office along with admission fee etc. and required certificate in original in case of selection of the child


REGISTRATION :


A form is available from the school office which must submitted within 3 days from the date of issue of form. Please note the registration fee is not refundable or not transferable and the registration does not in any way guarantee admission.


  • A copy of birth certificate/ Aadhar card must be attached with the registration form.
  • Students who have attended other school need to submit a photocopy of the report card of the last year from the preview school.

AT THE TIME OF ADMISSION :


Every parent is required to submit the following documents at the time of admission failing that the admission will be treated as provisional and is liable to be cancelled after the expiry of the time determined by ?the school.


  • A Birth certificate must be furnished for class Nursery, K.G. and 1st classes.
  • Transfer certificate/school leaving certificate from class II onwards duly countersigned by the District Education Officer.
  • Personal details except phone number & address of the student once entered at the time of admission will ?not be changed.
  • Any student granted admission at any period of the academic session will have to pay full fee as applicable to students who have taken admission in the beginning.
  • Four passport size coloured photo must be deposit along with admission form.
